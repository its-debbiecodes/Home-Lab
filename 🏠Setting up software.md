# Building My Home Lab 🏠
I just created a computer… inside my computer.
And yes, I was one click away from breaking everything 😭

**Building my Home Lab — Pt 1**

I’ve heard over and over that building a home lab is one of the best ways to get into IT support (alongside my Google IT Support Certificate)… so I finally started.

**Step 1:** Downloaded virtualisation software — Oracle VirtualBox
**Step 2:** Downloaded an OS — Ubuntu Desktop ISO

Then I set up my virtual machine:

* RAM: 2048MB
* Disk: 20GB
* Name: IT-lab-client-01
* OS: Linux

I powered it on and… boom — Ubuntu started booting inside my computer.

Felt like a genius for about 2 minutes.

Then disaster.

Black screen. Just a cursor staring at me like *“you thought you knew tech?”*

Quick Google search, panic clicking… then:
Machine → Reset.

And just like that — it worked.

I was staring at a full desktop… inside my actual computer.

Had to stop there for today, but I already know this is where the real learning starts — breaking things and figuring out how to fix them.

**Conclusion:**
Sometimes progress looks like messing everything up… then figuring out how to recover.
